## About this project 

This project is ongoing and involves supporting the development and execution of community-based initiatives for the kitchen kitchen and community center De Sering. As a freelance project manager, I support  operations, volunteer coordination, and implementation planning. I am closely involved in the creation of new initatives that are closely tied to operations, including involving volunteers in  financial reporting and initiating volunteer shifts at De Sering's second location, Sering Centraal. The work includes helping organize activities, supporting logistics, and contributing to the day-to-day execution of projects.
